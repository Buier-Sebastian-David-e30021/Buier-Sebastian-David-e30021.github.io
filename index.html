Performance Analysis

Acceleration Analysis

The C 200 CDI delivers respectable performance for a diesel-powered sedan with a 0-62 mph time of 10.4 seconds. While not built for outright speed, the car offers excellent everyday drivability thanks to its substantial torque output of 270 Nm available from just 1600 rpm.

0-62 mph (0-100 km/h) 10.4 seconds
50-75 mph (80-120 km/h) 10.8 seconds
Quarter mile 17.6 seconds

Efficiency Focus

The Mercedes C 200 CDI strikes an excellent balance between performance and efficiency. The 2.2-liter turbocharged diesel engine delivers impressive fuel economy figures, especially on highway journeys where it can achieve over 44 mpg (5.3 l/100km).

Fuel tank range (highway) ~800 miles (1300 km)
CO₂ efficiency rating B

About The Mercedes C 200 CDI

German Engineering Excellence

The W204 generation of the Mercedes-Benz C-Class represented a significant leap forward in the compact executive sedan segment. Launched in 2007, this model featured more dynamic styling, improved build quality, and enhanced driving dynamics compared to its predecessor.

Premium Interior Experience

The interior of the C-Class W204 features high-quality materials throughout, with intuitive controls and excellent ergonomics. The cabin design draws inspiration from the flagship S-Class, bringing luxury amenities and technologies to the compact executive segment.

The C 200 CDI Model

The C 200 CDI variant features Mercedes-Benz's reliable 2.2-liter four-cylinder turbodiesel engine, which balances performance and efficiency. With 136 horsepower and 270 Nm of torque, this powerplant provides adequate acceleration and excellent fuel economy. The engine is paired with a smooth-shifting 6-speed manual transmission, directing power to the rear wheels in true Mercedes fashion.

Known for its solid build quality and refined road manners, the C 200 CDI offers excellent long-distance comfort combined with respectable handling. The suspension setup strikes a good balance between comfort and control, soaking up road imperfections while maintaining composure through corners.

Standard equipment on the C 200 CDI includes climate control, partial power seats, and Mercedes' audio system. Safety features were class-leading at the time, with multiple airbags, electronic stability control, and Mercedes' Pre-Safe system available. The C 200 CDI represents an excellent entry point into Mercedes-Benz ownership, offering the brand's renowned quality and prestige in an efficient and practical package.
